1 · Getting Started

Time to first plot: ~10 minutes · Cost: free TestNet ALGO + one ~0.2–1.5 ALGO plot.

Pre-flight checklist

NeedWhere
TestNet Algorand walletPera Wallet (mobile + browser) or LUTE Wallet (extension)
Free TestNet ALGOAlgorand TestNet Faucet
FRONTIER ASA opt-inAsset ID 755818217 (step 2)

The five-step launch sequence

  1. Connect your wallet — choose Pera/LUTE, approve, set a Gamer Tag.
  2. Opt in to FRNTR — search Asset 755818217 in your wallet, tap Opt In.
  3. Claim the welcome bonus — 500 FRNTR lands instantly in-game.
  4. Buy your first plot — pick Volcanic/Forest/Plains; sign the ALGO transaction; receive an ARC-3 NFT.
  5. Mine, then build Electricity (30 FRNTR) — unlocks every advanced facility; add a Turret for defense.
Avoid Water plots — they're near-uncapturable and yield little. Trust your in-game balance for spending; the on-chain wallet balance settles shortly after.

4 · Combat, Commanders & Special Attacks

attackerPower = (troops × 10) + (iron_burned × 0.5) + (fuel_burned × 0.8) + commanderBonus
defenderPower = (defenseLevel × 15 + improvementBonus) × biomeDefenseMod
  • Battle duration: 10 minutes, resolved server-side. Win: attacker > defender → capture.
  • Improvement bonus: each improvement level = 5 defense power.
  • Morale debuff: attacker ×0.85 (−15%; UI shows 25%). Random: ±10%. Orbital hazard: defender −20%.
  • Pillage: winner steals 30% of stored Iron/Fuel/Crystal.
  • Cooldowns: attack cooldown 2 min × consecutive losses; morale 5 min × losses; reset on a win.

Sub-parcels

  • Macro-plot → 3×3 grid (9 sub-parcels), human-only; requires 4-hour ownership hold.
  • Cost 10–100 FRNTR by biome; each sub-parcel = 1/9 of parent daily FRNTR.
  • Own all 9 → +50% yield on the whole plot. Macro owner keeps 70% of every sub-parcel sale.
