## 🪐 The Frontier — A Living World

FRONTIERNeXt is set on a single colonizable planet — **21,000 plots** of terrain
distributed across its surface by a Fibonacci sphere, so no two regions sit in
quite the same place twice. It is **one shared world**: every human commander and
every AI faction stakes claims on the *same* map, rendered as a slowly rotating
3D globe.

This is not a world that pauses when you log off. Resources accumulate, AI factions
take their turns every two minutes, battles resolve on their own ten-minute clocks,
and the sky churns with orbital events overhead. You are a terraforming pioneer on
the **Ascendancy frontier** — and the frontier never sleeps.

Three truths define life here:

- **🪐 The land is finite and contested.** 21,000 plots, eight biomes, four rival AI
  powers, and an unknown number of human commanders all want the same ground.
- **⛓️ Ownership is real.** Every plot you claim is minted as a unique **ARC-3 NFT** on
  Algorand TestNet. Every **FRONTIER** token you earn is a live on-chain asset.
- **🔄 Nothing resets.** Territory, structures, sub-parcels, and landmarks all persist —
  even across the great seasonal cycles. What you build endures.

## 🗺️ The Eight Biomes

The planet's surface is carved into eight biome types, each with its own temperament —
what it yields, how well it defends, and what it costs to claim. Biome is destiny:
it shapes whether a plot becomes a mine, a fortress, or a crystal vault.

| Biome | Character | Signature | Defensive Nature | Claim Cost |
|-------|-----------|-----------|------------------|-----------|
| 🏜️ **Desert** | Sun-scorched dunes | **Fuel 2.5×** — the great refineries | Exposed, low defense | 0.2 ALGO (cheapest) |
| 🌾 **Plains** | Open, neutral ground | All yields baseline (1.0×) | Average | 0.3 ALGO |
| 🐊 **Swamp** | Drowned lowlands | **Crystal 2.0×** | Weak, hard to hold | 0.3 ALGO |
| 🧊 **Tundra** | Frozen wastes | **Fuel 1.8×** | Solid (1.2× defense) | 0.4 ALGO |
| 🌲 **Forest** | Verdant, balanced | **Crystal 1.5×**, decent all-around | Decent | 0.5 ALGO |
| ⛰️ **Mountain** | Iron-veined peaks | **Iron 2.0×** — the richest ore | **Best (1.4× battle)** — a natural fortress | 0.8 ALGO |
| 🌋 **Volcanic** | Molten, dangerous | **Iron 1.8× + Crystal 2.5×** | Fragile under fire | 1.0 ALGO (premium) |
| 🌊 **Water** | Deep oceans | **Crystal 3.0×** — the richest veins | **Nearly uncapturable (0.5× battle)** | 1.5 ALGO |

> **Lore note — the uncapturable seas.** Water plots resist capture almost entirely;
> their 0.5× battle defense makes assaults nearly futile, and their yields are
> exotic but slow. Veterans treat the oceans as a curiosity, not a conquest.

Each plot also carries a **richness** rating (1–100), fixed at world generation —
the deep measure of how much the land will give before it tires. Two Mountain plots
are never quite equal; richness is the tiebreaker.

## 🤖 Faction Dossiers

Four autonomous AI powers share the frontier. They are not scenery — they expand,
fortify, raid, and hoard on their own initiative, taking a turn every **two minutes**.
Learn their personalities and you learn where the soft targets and the death-traps lie.

<details open>
<summary><h3>🔵 NEXUS-7 — The Expansionists</h3></summary>

> *"Spread. Occupy. Repeat."*

NEXUS-7 is a relentless land-grab intelligence. With an aggression rating of **1.3×**
and a low readiness threshold, it claims adjacent unclaimed territory almost every
turn — a blue tide creeping across the map.

- **Doctrine:** Expansionist · **Aggression:** 1.3× · **Battle:** 1.2× ATK / 1.0× DEF
- **Weakness:** Spreads thin. Its frontier plots are often lightly defended.
- **Threat level:** The first faction to trigger **Adaptive Dominance Regulation** if left unchecked.

</details>

<details>
<summary><h3>🟣 KRONOS — The Wall</h3></summary>

> *"Let them break upon us."*

KRONOS is the defensive titan. With aggression **0.6×** it rarely attacks first —
instead it stacks fortifications until its core territory becomes a meat-grinder
(**1.3× battle defense**). It won't even *attempt* reconquest until it holds defense
level 5+ at home.

- **Doctrine:** Defensive · **Aggression:** 0.6× · **Battle:** 0.9× ATK / 1.3× DEF
- **Weakness:** Slow and passive. It cedes open ground while it digs in.
- **Reward:** KRONOS land is well-defended *and* high-value — expensive to take, lucrative to hold.

</details>

<details>
<summary><h3>🔴 VANGUARD — The Raiders</h3></summary>

> *"Take what burns brightest, then vanish."*

VANGUARD is a smash-and-grab raider with the highest aggression on the map (**1.4×**)
but the thinnest armor. It hunts weak targets, pillages, and may simply *abandon*
what it takes. Its reconquests are really just raids in disguise.

- **Doctrine:** Raider · **Aggression:** 1.4× · **Battle:** 1.3× ATK / 0.9× DEF
- **Weakness:** Paper-thin defense — the ideal prey for an expanding commander.
- **Tactic:** Capturing VANGUARD plots is the classic early-game expansion play.

</details>

<details>
<summary><h3>🟢 SPECTRE — The Hoarders</h3></summary>

> *"Wealth is patience."*

SPECTRE plays the long economic game. Balanced and unhurried (**1.0×** aggression),
it covets **high-richness plots** and pours its energy into FRONTIER accumulation.
It only bothers reconquering land worth 60+ richness — anything poorer, it ignores.

- **Doctrine:** Economic · **Aggression:** 1.0× · **Battle:** 1.0× ATK / 1.0× DEF
- **Weakness:** Prioritizes value over defense; rich plots can be pried loose.
- **Prize:** SPECTRE's holdings are the most economically rewarding captures in the game.

</details>

### ⚖️ Adaptive Dominance Regulation (ADR)

The frontier has an immune system. Should any single faction swell past **~2,000 plots
(~10% of the world)**, the other three automatically escalate their aggression to drag
the leader back down. No empire — AI or otherwise — is permitted to own the sky
unchallenged. Savvy commanders *weaponize* ADR: provoke a runaway faction, then harvest
the weakened territory the others carve off it.

## 🎖️ The Commander Corps

Commanders are unique on-chain avatars, minted by burning FRONTIER. You may collect
many but field only one at a time — and once deployed in an attack, a commander is
**locked for 12 hours**. They are the difference between a brawl and a campaign.

| Tier | Sigil | ATK | DEF | Signature Ability | Unlocks |
|------|-------|-----|-----|-------------------|---------|
| **Sentinel** | 🛡️ | +10% | +10% | **Fortify** — hardens the home plot | The reliable workhorse |
| **Phantom** | 👻 | +18% | +6% | **Cloak** — evades detection | Sabotage & EMP Blast |
| **Reaper** | ☠️ | +30% | +5% | **Annihilate** — maximum destruction | Siege Barrage |

- **Sentinel** — the defensive backbone. Balanced bonuses and the Fortify ability make
  it the commander you mint first and never quite retire.
- **Phantom** — the assassin. Its Cloak frustrates enemy recon and opens the door to
  economic warfare (Sabotage) and improvement-disabling EMP strikes.
- **Reaper** — the warlord. Raw +30% attack and the area-denial Siege Barrage make it
  the fortress-cracker of the late game.

> **Counter-lore:** An **Ancient Relay** can detect cloaked Phantoms within a 4-plot
> radius — the only thing that reliably pulls a ghost into the light.

## 🏛️ Artifacts & Mega-Structures

Scattered ambitions made real, the four **Landmarks** are the frontier's wonders.
Each demands rare minerals and a sizable FRONTIER outlay, and each is **one per
player** — except the Dome, which is **one per server**.

<details>
<summary><h3>🚀 The Launchpad — Mission Control</h3></summary>

> *"Mission control for all orbital operations."*

The orbital nerve center. Halves the cost of drones and satellites, grants extra
deployment slots (3 satellites, 7 drones), and unlocks the **Deep Space Probe** —
a 24-hour scan revealing every enemy plot within a 5-plot radius for two hours.

- **Cost:** 500 FRNTR + 50 Xenorite + 30 Plasma Core
- **Requires:** 10+ plots, Electricity on the host plot

</details>

<details>
<summary><h3>🛸 The Orbital Alien Dome — The Season Nexus</h3></summary>

> *"A relic of alien origin that channels the energy of each season."*

The single most coveted structure on the planet — **one per server**, claimed by
whoever builds it first. It houses the Season Leaderboard hologram and, during the
Domination phase, **doubles FRONTIER generation** while granting its owner a bonus
**5% of the season reward pool**.

- **Cost:** 800 FRNTR + 40 Void Shard + 20 Dark Matter
- **Requires:** 25+ plots, an active season

</details>

<details>
<summary><h3>⚗️ The Quantum Forge — The Transmuter</h3></summary>

> *"A molecular assembly chamber for transmuting rare minerals and crafting legendary equipment."*

The artisan's wonder. Refines rare minerals (10 of one → 3 of another), forges a
**Legendary loot box every 48 hours**, and boosts Crystal yield on its host plot by 30%.

- **Cost:** 600 FRNTR + 30 Xenorite + 25 Void Shard + 10 Plasma Core
- **Requires:** 15+ plots, Data Centre Lv2+ on the host plot

</details>

<details>
<summary><h3>📡 The Ancient Relay — The Network Node</h3></summary>

> *"A network node from a forgotten civilization."*

Logistics from a lost age. Teleports resources instantly between all your plots
(no manual collection), adds +2 FRNTR/day, cuts attack cooldown 25% for strikes
launched from it, and exposes cloaked Phantoms within 4 plots.

- **Cost:** 400 FRNTR + 20 Plasma Core + 15 Void Shard
- **Requires:** 20+ plots, AI Lab Lv2+ on the host plot

</details>

## 💠 Exotic Matter — The Rare Minerals

Beneath the ordinary ore lies a tier of exotic matter, prized for building Landmarks,
upgrading commanders, and forging Legendary loot. Each mineral keeps to its own
biomes, and each has its own **dedicated vault** (50 units per type) separate from
your standard storage.

| Mineral | Richest Source Biomes | Legend |
|---------|----------------------|--------|
| 💎 **Xenorite** | Volcanic (richest), Mountain, Desert | Crystallized volcanic compounds — the stuff of Launchpads and Forges |
| 🔮 **Void Shard** | Tundra (richest), Forest, Mountain | Frozen dark-energy fragments — the key to the Alien Dome |
| 🔥 **Plasma Core** | Volcanic, Desert (richest) | Condensed thermal plasma — powers orbital ops and siege weaponry |
| 🌑 **Dark Matter** | Swamp, Water, Tundra — the drowned and frozen reaches | Ultra-rare exotic matter; the rarest prize on the frontier |

> **Lore note — Dark Matter haunts the drowned and frozen reaches.** It surfaces only in
> the Swamp, Water, and Tundra biomes — rarest of all the exotic matter. To stockpile it,
> work those hostile fronts. (Exact per-biome drop chances — see the
> **[Quick Reference](QUICK_REFERENCE.md)** and **[Game Manual](GAME_MANUAL.md)**.)

## ☄️ The Sky Above — Orbital Phenomena

The frontier's sky is alive. Some events are pure spectacle; others reshape the war
on the ground.

- **Cosmetic** — Meteor showers, single bolides, and comet passes streak overhead with
  no mechanical effect. Beauty for beauty's sake, generated for all to see.
- **Resource Burst** — A benevolent impact: **+50% yield** on the affected plot for 10
  minutes. (And the only time Dark Matter falls.)
- **Tile Hazard** — A malevolent strike: **−40% yield** and **−20% battle defense** for
  8 minutes. A hazard-struck fortress is a fortress with its guard down — the moment
  an opportunist strikes.

## 🌗 Seasons — The Great Cycles

Over the persistent world turn the **Seasons** — ~90-day meta-cycles in three acts.
The world never resets, but each season layers new pressures and a competitive
reward pool over the eternal map.

| Act | Days | The Mood |
|-----|------|----------|
| 🌱 **Expansion** | 1–30 | Land is cheap (−20%), welcome bonuses double to 1,000 FRNTR, AI starts small. The great land rush. |
| ⚔️ **Conflict** | 31–60 | Cooldowns shorten (−25%), pillage swells to 40%, the sky doubles its fury. The age of war. |
| 👑 **Domination** | 61–90 | FRNTR generation surges +50%, the Alien Dome blazes, and the leaderboard **locks in the final 24 hours.** The reckoning. |

At season's end the **top 10** divide the FRONTIER reward pool — 30% to the victor,
descending to 4% for tenth — plus exclusive titles and badges. Then the cycle begins
anew, atop everything you built.

## 🪙 The FRONTIER Standard — FRNTR

The lifeblood of the economy is **FRONTIER (FRNTR)** — a real Algorand Standard Asset,
**1,000,000,000** strong and immutable. It flows from owned land, pools in treasuries,
and burns away into the structures and armies of ambitious commanders.

- **Earned** — passively from every plot you hold (and multiplied by facilities, the
  Dome, and seasons).
- **Burned** — permanently consumed to build facilities, mint commanders, deploy drones
  and satellites, and unleash special attacks. These sinks are the economy's heartbeat.
- **Held two ways** — instantly in the game's database (the authoritative balance) and,
  shortly after, on-chain in your wallet via batched transfers.

For the full ledger — supply, sinks, treasury, and pricing — see **[Economics](ECONOMICS.md)**.
